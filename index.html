About Us

A place where you can relax, study, and drink good milk tea. We are a pipe dream turned into reality: the first coworking cafe of Las Vegas. Our chic yet cozy atmosphere is perfect for spending the day, whether chatting with friends or finishing your latest project. We offer an assortment of sweets for every person - from milk tea to honey toast.

We are a coworking space: a collaborative environment for you and your peers. Whether lounging around or studying, we offer a unique space for you to be the best you. We offer options with rental rooms for those who desire privacy in a single or group setting.

Mission Statement

To provide a relaxing and revitalizing cafe experience to our community through a collaborative space.
